# Islamic Golden Age Rebrand — Design & Content Plan

A historically grounded re-skin of the whole game (currently a Clash Royale
clone) into an **Islamic military-history** strategy game. Gameplay/stats stay
identical; only **names, art, and flavor** change. This doc is the working map
from every existing card/tower/building to a real (or folkloric) equivalent.

---

## 1. Vision & era

**Anchor era:** the Islamic **Golden Age through the Crusader/Mamluk period and
into the early gunpowder age** (~8th–15th c. CE) — Abbasid → Fatimid → Ayyubid
(Saladin) → Mamluk → early Ottoman. This span is chosen because it covers the
full tech curve the card roster needs:

- **Naphtha / "Greek fire" era** → the fire units (Naffatun, fire-pots).
- **Siege era** → mangonels/trebuchets (manjaniq), citadels, ribats.
- **Cavalry golden age** → Faris, Mamluks, camel corps, cataphracts.
- **Gunpowder dawn** → Janissaries (arquebus), great bombards (Constantinople).

**Working title ideas:** *Citadel Royale*, *Sands of the Caliphate*, *Manjaniq*,
*Faris*, *Bayt al-Harb (House of War)*.

---

## 2. Cultural & religious sensitivity (READ FIRST — non-negotiable)

This theme must be celebratory and respectful. Hard rules:

- **No depiction of the Prophet ﷺ, any prophets, companions as combat units**
  (a famous general may appear only as a respectful named "legendary" portrait,
  never killed/destroyed on screen — see §9, treat as optional & careful).
- **No Qur'anic verses or the name of Allah on weapons, armor, or destructible
  objects.** Decorative calligraphy = generic poetry/geometric *girih* only.
- **The King's tower is a CITADEL/PALACE (Qaṣr / Qalʿa), never a mosque.** Do
  not make a place of worship a military target or an attacking unit. Minarets
  may appear as **watchtowers/bastions** (historically they doubled as such) but
  keep crescents as decorative finials, not targets to "destroy."
- **No djinn or angels as troops** (theological beings). Use humans, real
  animals (horses, camels, elephants, falcons), and machines. Folkloric
  *secular* creatures from *1001 Nights* (Rukh/Roc, Simurgh) are acceptable for
  the "fantasy flyer" slots.
- **Reframe "magic" as Golden-Age science** — alchemy, naphtha, engineering,
  optics, astronomy. Historically the Islamic world *led* these fields, so this
  is both respectful and accurate. No sorcery/necromancy.
- **No undead.** Skeletons → militia levies (see mapping).

## 5. Troops (all 26)

Format: **current → Arabic · translit · English** — *historical basis* (role is
unchanged). Grouped by gameplay role.

### Melee infantry
- **Knight** → فارس · *Fāris* · Cavalier — balanced sword-and-shield man-at-arms (on foot or light horse).
- **Mini P.E.K.K.A** → مبارز · *Mubāriz* · Champion Duelist — the warrior who fights single combat before a battle; fast, high single-target damage.
- **Valkyrie** → سيّافة · *Sayyāfa* · Whirling Swordswoman — Mamluk/Berber axe-or-saber dancer; spin = area sweep. (Female warrior, e.g. inspired by Amazigh fighters.)
- **P.E.K.K.A** → فارس مدجّج · *Fāris Mudajjaj* · Cataphract — fully-armored heavy cavalryman, slowest/heaviest hitter.
- **Mega Knight** → أمير المماليك · *Amīr al-Mamālīk* · Mamluk Champion — elite armored amir with a great mace; "jump" = thunderous mounted charge/slam.

### Cavalry & raiders
- **Prince** → فارس بالرمح · *Fāris bi-l-Rumḥ* · Lancer Knight — heavy lance charge (already given a tournament lance). Optional legendary skin: a celebrated general (handle per §2/§9).
- **Hog Rider** → هجّان · *Hajjān* · Camel Raider — fast Arab camel-corps raider that rushes the bastions.

### Archers & ranged
- **Archers** → رُماة · *Rumāt* · Archers — the classic two-bowmen card (Rogue model already shows its baked crossbow).
- **Magic Archer** → كماندار · *Kamāndār* · Master Bowman — elite Persian marksman whose arrow pierces a whole line.
- **Princess** → خولة · *Khawla* · Warrior Maiden — long-range fire-arrow heroine (inspired by Khawla bint al-Azwar, a real female warrior). Keep flaming arrow.

### Fire / "alchemy" (Golden-Age science, NOT magic)
- **Firecracker** → نفّاط · *Naffāṭ* · Naphtha Thrower — real Islamic incendiary trooper spitting fire-sparks.
- **Wizard** → كيميائي · *al-Kīmiyāʾī* · Alchemist — hurls incendiary fire-pots (qārūrat naft); the fire-orb attack stays.
- **Electro Wizard** → نفّاط الصاعق · *Naffāṭ Ṣāʿiq* · Flash Naphtha-Siphon — a siphon crewman whose blast **dazzles/stuns** and splashes to 2 foes (reskin the "lightning" as a blinding flash/fire-arc). *Hardest to keep realistic; this is the recommended framing.*
- **Ice Wizard** → ساقي الثلج · *Sāqī al-Thalj* · Snow-Bearer / Caltrop Sapper — slows enemies. Two framings: (a) mountain-snow chiller (the medieval ice/sharbat trade was real), or (b) a sapper scattering **caltrops (ḥasak)** that slow movement. Pick one and reskin the projectile to match.
- **Executioner** → جلّاد · *Jallād* · Axeman — returning war-axe (cf. Ottoman *Baltacı* axemen).

### Heavy / siege movers
- **Giant** → فيل الحرب · *Fīl al-Ḥarb* · War Elephant — slow building-targeting tank (Ghaznavid war elephants). Big visual upgrade opportunity.
- **Royal Giant** → حامل المدفع · *Ḥāmil al-Madfaʿ* · Bombardier — a giant bearing an early great-cannon (Ottoman *Şāhī*/Orban bombard, Siege of Constantinople 1453); long-range tower-breaker.

### Swarms / levies (replaces all undead)
- **Skeletons** → أحداث · *Aḥdāth* · City Militia — cheap volunteer levy swarm.
- **Skeleton Army** → حشد الأحداث · *Ḥashd al-Aḥdāth* · Militia Horde — the big swarm.
- **Witch** (summoner) → نقيب · *Naqīb* · Recruiting Officer — beats a war-drum to summon militia (replaces necromancy with mustering). Alt: *Tabbāl* (drummer).
- **Bats** → خفافيش · *Khafāfīsh* · Bats — keep as real animals (cheap flyer swarm), or **desert swifts**.

### Flyers (folklore & falconry — see §2)
- **Baby Dragon** → فرخ الرُخّ · *Farkh al-Rukhkh* · Roc Hatchling — young giant bird from *1001 Nights*; flying fire-breath → fiery screech/embers. (Alt: *Sīmurgh* chick.)
- **Gargoyles** → صقور · *Ṣuqūr* · War Falcons — trained falconry birds; fast flyer pair.
- **Minions** → سرب الصقور · *Sirb al-Ṣuqūr* · Falcon Flock — ranged flyer swarm (talon/fire-pellet attack).
- **Balloon** → طائرة نفط · *Ṭāʾira Naft* · Fire-Kite — a war-kite carrying a naphtha bomb to drop on bastions. *Alt (more realistic, ground unit): Dabbāba (mobile siege tower) — but that breaks the "flyer" niche; recommend the fire-kite/Roc-with-fire-pot to keep an air unit.*

## 7. Art direction

- **Palette:** keep the existing `theme.ts` (turquoise, teal, deep blue, gold,
  terracotta, cream, sand) — Moorish/Persian tilework jewel tones + gold.
- **Patterns:** *girih* geometric strapwork + arabesque vegetal scrolls for card
  frames, HUD, tower trims; eight-point stars (khatam) as accents.
- **Architecture:** horseshoe & pointed arches, muqarnas (honeycomb) vaulting on
  towers, onion/ribbed domes, glazed tilework, carved screens (mashrabiya).
- **Calligraphy:** decorative *kufic/thuluth*-style flourishes — **non-sacred
  text only** (unit names, generic mottos). 
- **Card rarity frames:** bronze/silver/gold/turquoise-enamel tiers.
- **Audio:** oud, ney, qanun, riq, and **naqqara war-drums**; takbir-free battle
  cries; victory = nashid-style instrumental fanfare (instrumental, no sacred
  lyrics).
- **Calendar/flavor:** name seasons after Hijri months; arenas after real
  frontier cities (Damascus, Córdoba, Cairo, Baghdad, Granada, Acre).

## 8. Implementation roadmap (maps to the codebase)

The game already has the bones for this — there's an **`ARABIC` theme** that
dresses rigs in turbans and the arena in crescents (default). Build on it.

1. **Naming layer (cheap, high impact).** Add an Arabic display-name + subtitle
   per card. Keep `CardId`s the same internally; add a `theme`-aware label map
   (e.g. `cardNames.ts`) consumed by HUD/deck-builder/card-info. Renders the new
   names without touching sim/balance.
2. **Resource & UI copy.** Elixir → *Naft*; recolor the meter; relabel King →
   Sultan, towers → Qalʿa/Burj, crowns → banners.
3. **Building/spell reskins.** Tombstone→Ribat (militia, not bones),
   Cannon→Manjaniq, Collector→Khan; spell VFX recolor (zap→flash, freeze→caltrops).
4. **Character art pass.** The hand-built rigs already turban up under `ARABIC`.
   Add the distinctive silhouettes: camel for Hajjān, elephant for Fīl, falcons
   for Ṣuqūr, Roc for the dragon, bombard for the Royal Giant, drum for Naqīb.
   (The GLB western models stay confined to the *normal* theme — never let them
   bleed into the Arabic theme; see `glbModels.ts` gating.)
5. **Arena & towers.** Citadel keep, archer bastions, oasis canal, arched
   bridges, tilework floor, decorative (non-target) minaret finials.
6. **Polish.** Audio pack, girih UI frames, Hijri seasons, city-named arenas.

> Stats, costs, hit-speeds, and balance are untouched throughout — this is a
> re-skin, so the existing tests and sim stay green.

## 9. Stretch / optional

- **Legendary heroes** (cosmetic skins on existing cards): celebrated *generals*
  — e.g. Saladin (Ayyubid), Khalid ibn al-Walid, Tariq ibn Ziyad, Baybars.
  Handle with dignity: portraits/leaders, never shown being "destroyed"; offer
  as a togglable respectful homage, and be ready to drop any that feel off.
- **Campaign:** historically-flavored battles (frontier raids, a siege, naval at
  a port) — flavor only, same sim.
- **Naval set (future cards):** *Shīnī* war-galley, *Ḥarrāqa* fire-ship — fits
  the naphtha theme if you ever add water lanes.
