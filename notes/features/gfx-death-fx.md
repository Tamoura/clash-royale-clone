# Graphics: per-card death effects

- Pure `deathStyle(cardId)` mapping (tested): bones for skeletons,
  purple sparks for the P.E.K.K.A family, deflate for the balloon,
  classic puff otherwise.
- scene3d implements the three new effects: tossed bone shards with
  gravity, radial electric sparks + small blast, and a spiraling
  shrinking envelope.
