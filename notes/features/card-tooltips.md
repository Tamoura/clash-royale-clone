# Card stat tooltips

- New tested pure helper cardStatLines(id): kind/rarity line,
  HP/damage, trait line (speed, range, flies, hits air, splash,
  charge, jumps river...), powers line (summons, death bomb,
  elixir income, lifetime).
- Hovering a hand card shows a gold-framed tooltip above the hand
  with name, cost, and those lines.
