# Expressive faces (user-approved design round)

Approved from the design round: faces only (hero redesigns, glow
fix, studio polish were proposed and NOT approved — do not apply).

- addEyes -> full face: dark eyeliner rim + white sclera + pupil,
  mood-angled brows (brave/angry/cute/wicked/calm via BROW_TILT),
  smile (cute) or line mouth. All parts named for tests.
- Moods: knight/musketeer/prince brave; archers/tower-princess
  cute; giant/wizard/tower-king calm; valkyrie/hog-rider angry;
  witch wicked. Skeleton/gargoyle/dragon/pekkas keep their custom
  eyes.
