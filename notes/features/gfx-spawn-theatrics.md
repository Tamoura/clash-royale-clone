# Graphics: spawn theatrics

- Pure `spawnStyle(cardId)` (tested): skeletons "rise", others "pop".
- Risers emerge through a dark necromantic disc with a green rim
  ring (pairs with the witch's summons); the body starts below the
  ground plane and eases up, so it reads as clawing out of the dirt.
- Poppers keep the easeOutBack bounce and now also get a deploy
  dust puff at the moment of arrival.
