# Freeze (spell)

4-elixir CR classic: no damage, 4s stun in a 3-tile radius (towers
included, like CR). Pure reuse of the zap stun mechanic — the card
definition is the entire sim change.

- 3D: icy flash + frost circle that lingers for the stun duration
  with growing ice shards. 2D: six-arm snowflake card art.
- Audio: glassy descending shimmer.
